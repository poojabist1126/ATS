These are the command lines.
show <product | employee> - to show products or employees details in table
delete <product | employee> <id> - to delete
add <product | employee> - to add new details
update <product | employee> - to update details
login <admin | none> - to login, type nothing after login keyword to login as customer or type admin after login keyword to login as an admin
signup - to signup as customer
help - to look commands
